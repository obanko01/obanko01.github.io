Oluseye Bankole - Personal Biography

Early Life and Education

Commonly referred to my close friends as banky, I was born in the city of Lagos to a family of 5. I didn't come along though, hence my birthname Taiwo. I have a lovely twin sister and we make the best twin combo ever.
